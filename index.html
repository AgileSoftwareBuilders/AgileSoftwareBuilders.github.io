Service

Hire a skilled agile team

In most innovative projects, little is known up front and one has to adapt along the way. With Agile software builders, you can hire a skilled agile team. We use short development cycles (sprints) with working software all the time to ensure you won't be surprised. Each sprint, you can reevaluate and reprioritise features, we'll adapt instantly. Focussing on business value, we can come to a valuable solution fast, and one that needs less features than initially you might think you need.

  • Less is more

    Don't just tell us what you want, but explain us why you want it. Together, we identify key features and determine priorities based on business value and risk. Features with little value can be postponed or cut away. This way, we can come to a valuable solution faster and cheaper, and one that needs less features than initially you think you need.

  • Short feedback cycles with working software

    We use short development cycles (sprints) of typically 2 weeks. Prior to the start of each cycle, you can reconsider the features and reshuffle their priority based on their current value for you. At the end of each cycle, we show you working software that is fully tested.

  • You say when

    We work on a sprint-per-sprint basis. We go as far as you want us to go. After each sprint, you can evaluate whether enough value is realised to release the software or whether you are ready to take over the ownership.

  • Incorporate your people on our team

    Optionally, you can have your people participate in our team. They'll receive immersive training on our agile way-of-working and can confidently take over the software ownership after the project.
